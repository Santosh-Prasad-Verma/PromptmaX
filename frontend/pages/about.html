Build the next generation of AI prompts

PromptX helps developers, researchers, and content creators craft better AI prompts through intelligent enhancement and quality analysis.

Our Mission

PromptX was created to solve a fundamental problem: most prompts sent to AI systems are vague, incomplete, or poorly structured. This leads to inconsistent outputs, wasted tokens, and frustrated users.

We built a 9-stage neural pipeline that transforms raw inputs into precision-engineered instructions. The system analyzes intent, adds missing context, applies best practices from the CREATE algorithm, and scores quality across 6 dimensions.

What makes us different

9-Stage Pipeline

Every prompt passes through validation, intent detection, context building, template matching, refinement, fact-checking, and quality scoring.

Multi-Model Fallback

Primary processing via Gemini 2.0 Flash with automatic fallback to NVIDIA Minimax and Groq Llama 3 for maximum reliability.

Quality Scoring

Six-dimensional analysis (clarity, specificity, structure, context, constraints, format) with actionable improvement suggestions.
